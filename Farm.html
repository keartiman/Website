Keartiman Farms

Keartiman Farms was founded by Mr.Tharoor Raghavan in 1997, at Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu to train the small and marginal farmers in organic farming practices that are eco-sensitive and do not destroy the earthâ€™s resources. The Farm advocates integrated organic farming as a more sustainable alternative to modern agricultural practices which require unsustainable inputs like high-cost fertilizer and pesticides. Keartiman Farms aims at training small and marginal farmers in organic farming practices with the objective of improving the economic status of farmers through the marketing of organic produce to urban customers. Vegetables, Fruits, cereals, pulses, millets and various other products are cultivated on a contract basis.
