Keartiman Matriculation Higher Secondary School

Brief History

Keartiman Matriculation and Higher Secondary School is a brainchild of the great visionary, Sri.T.Raghavan a philanthropist, industrialist and retired senior official at the Central Bureau of Investigation. His dream was to establish an English medium school and impart quality education to the children of Kanuvai, a rural area in Coimbatore in the 90â€™s.

The dream came into being in June, 1999 with 143 pupils. The strength has grown manifold since then to about 2700 students from Kindergarten to Std. XII. The unique feature of our school is that it fulfills the vision of its Founding father to provide affordable yet quality education. Apart from the management, our dedicated team of experienced and well qualified teachers ensure in materializing the dream.

Our School

Keartiman Matriculation Higher Secondary School is run by the Keartiman Trust. It is recognized by the Board of Matriculation schools under the Education Department of Tamilnadu and trains students for the X & XII Board exams. The medium of instruction is English, while Tamil is taught as second language and Hindi as optional.

The school has a glorious history of over 20 years to its credit. It is counted among the top educational institutions in Tamilnadu not only in terms of academics but for its contribution towards producing global citizens.Currently over 2700 children study in various classes of the school starting from kindergarten to XII grade. Apart from excellent academic support, the children also have access to world class facilities and faculty for sports and cultural activities.

The management ensures that the institution not only imparts knowledge but sees to the spiritual, moral & physical well-being of their pupils.
